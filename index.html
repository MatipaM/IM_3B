Studio Ghibli Data Visualisation Website

Welcome to Matipa Marufu's data visualisation website. This website will be based on the Studio Ghibli API. It will contain a section in the data art and data visualisation pages which will allow the user to select their favourite movie and in turn the page will generate corresponding information in a digestable yet aesthetic format to the user.

What makes Studio Ghibli stand out from other animation studios is their ability to build ultra realistic worlds which transport the viewer through their made up universe. Studio Ghibli's detailed environments, tend to be embedded in nature hence why I chose this background image. As it is a snapshot into what type of art the studio produces, and how they incorporate unique species/spirits into the 'human worlds'. I continued to use the blues and greens as seen in the background throughout the website, to create a link between environmental colours and the environments in the worlds built by Studio Ghibli.
